Types of Online Advertising

1. Display Ads

Display ads are graphical ads that appear on websites. They can be in the form of banners, images, or videos.

2. Search Engine Marketing (SEM)

SEM involves promoting a website by increasing its visibility on search engine results pages through paid advertising.

3. Social Media Advertising

Social media advertising involves creating and sharing content on social media platforms to promote a product or service.

4. Video Advertising

Video ads are promotional content displayed in online videos. They can be pre-roll, mid-roll, or post-roll ads.

5. Email Marketing

Email marketing involves sending targeted messages to a group of people via email to promote a product or service.
